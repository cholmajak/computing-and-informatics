About The Department

Our Journey

A Brief History

The Department of Computing and Informatics at Bugema University has a rich tradition of pioneering technological education. Our journey began with a vision to equip students with the necessary technical skills to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

2005

The department was initially established as the IT Department. It began by offering foundational courses in Information Technology to a small but dedicated cohort of students aspiring to enter the rapidly growing tech sector.

Evolution & Growth

To reflect the rapid advancements in technology and our broadening academic scope, the IT Department evolved into the modern Department of Computing and Informatics. The curriculum was continually expanded to encompass advanced fields like Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, and Cyber Security.

Present Day

Today, we stand as a pillar of technological innovation and academic excellence. We remain committed to shaping well-rounded, industry-ready professionals capable of creating sophisticated solutions to modern-day challenges.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education and conduct impactful research in computing and informatics, fostering a generation of innovators, ethical professionals, and leaders who can leverage technology for societal development.

Our Vision

To be a premier center of excellence in computing education and research, recognized globally for producing graduates who drive technological transformation and sustainable development.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by a strong commitment to core principles that define our educational environment and scholarly pursuits:

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing the boundaries of knowledge and collaboratively applying creative solutions to complex problems.
  • Excellence: Maintaining the absolute highest standards across teaching, research, and professional practices.
  • Integrity: Upholding strict ethical standards, transparency, and honesty in all computing endeavors and campus life.
  • Collaboration: Fostering dedicated teamwork and productive partnerships between students, faculty members, and the broader tech industry.
